Unfortunately the Player doesn't support ID3v2 Tags (...hey, it's not from me!).
So, if you wan't to display a File Info (like that one) when you click on the Question Mark, you've to prepare your MP3 first

 

Follow up these 5 Steps:

  1. Open your Explorer and search for your MP3 File to edit.
  2. Click it with your right Mouse Key.
  3. Select "Properties".
  4. Select "File Info" (or Summary - not sure what an english XP displays).
